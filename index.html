Manufacturing Unit

As a manufacturing-focused leather supplier, H.L.T. Leather Industry supports footwear production through dedicated processing areas from raw material inspection to final dispatch.

Raw Material Zone

Leather, lining, soles, and accessories are checked for grade, finish, and yield before production use.

Cutting & Preparation

Material preparation for uppers, lining, insole covering, and sole component fitting as per order specs.

Adhesive & Bonding Area

PU + hardener and neoprene adhesive systems are handled in controlled workflow for consistent bonding.

Sole & Heel Integration

Support for wedge, semi-heel, stiletto, pencil heel, neolite, and PVC last based production requirements.

Quality Check

Every batch is reviewed for cutting quality, finish consistency, and readiness for footwear assembly lines.

Packing & Dispatch

Bulk dispatch planning with order-wise packing and delivery schedules aligned with customer quantity needs.
